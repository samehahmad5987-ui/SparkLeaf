The Origin

A simple idea
that changed everything.

Spark Leaf was born from a simple idea: energy should feel good — before, during, and after you drink it.

We were tired of energy drinks loaded with sugar, artificial ingredients, and the crash that always followed. So we turned to something timeless and powerful: matcha. Known for its smooth, sustained energy and natural focus, matcha became the heart of Spark Leaf.

We blended premium matcha with thoughtfully selected natural ingredients to create refreshing flavors — each crafted to energize without overwhelm. Spark Leaf is about fueling your day with clarity, balance, and intention, whether you're powering through work, working out, or simply living fully.

We didn't just want to make another energy drink. We wanted to make the right one — the one that consumers actually feel good about buying, and that distributors can confidently stand behind.

The Journey

From idea to
market-ready brand.

💡
The Idea
Identifying the Gap
Founders Prince Ahmed and Sameh Ahmad recognized that the U.S. energy drink market lacked a premium, matcha-based alternative — a gap already being filled profitably in Europe and Asia. The research began.
🔬
R&D Phase
Research & Formula Development
Extensive market research backed by sources including Grand View Research and Mordor Intelligence. Formula development focused on premium matcha sourcing, optimal L-theanine ratios, and real fruit flavor integration — all with minimal additives and sugar.
🎨
Brand & Product
Three Flavors. One Identity.
Matcha Wildberry, Matcha Citrus, and Matcha Mango Passionfruit — three distinct profiles designed to cover broad consumer taste preferences while maintaining Spark Leaf's premium, natural positioning. Packaging built for shelf impact and social media visibility.
🚀
Now · 2025
Market-Ready. Seeking Partners.
Spark Leaf is ready to enter the U.S. market with a distribution strategy built on early-partner advantage. We're actively seeking distribution and investment partners who understand the category and want to grow with it.
📈
The Vision · 2026–2030
Scale Across the U.S.
Strategic expansion into retail, foodservice, gyms, and specialty channels. As matcha-based beverages continue rising in consumer preference, Spark Leaf is positioned to be the brand that defines the category in the American market — just as Perfect Ted did in the UK.
