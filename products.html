The Science

Why matcha is the
superior energy source.

For centuries, Japanese monks used matcha for sustained focus during long meditation. The science behind why it works is directly translatable to modern consumer energy needs.

⚡
Natural Caffeine
Energy
Provides natural, sustained energy. Its absorption is moderated by L-theanine — resulting in a smooth curve instead of a sharp spike and crash.
🧘
L-Theanine
Focus
Promotes alpha brain wave activity — the same state of calm alertness achieved during meditation. Completely eliminates jitters and anxiety associated with caffeine.
🛡️
EGCG Antioxidants
Wellness
Catechins, particularly EGCG, offer powerful cell protection. Matcha has 137x more EGCG than regular brewed green tea — a genuinely meaningful wellness differentiator.
🌱
Vitamins & Minerals
Nutrition
Vitamin C, K, B vitamins, potassium, iron, magnesium, selenium, zinc, and chromium. Real nutritional density that mainstream energy drinks simply don't offer.
