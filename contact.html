Common Questions

Frequently asked by
partners and investors.

What stage is Spark Leaf currently at?
Spark Leaf is in its early launch phase — product is developed, branding is complete, and we are actively establishing our first distribution partnerships and investor relationships in the U.S. market. This is the optimal time to move.
Are there exclusivity options for distributors?
Yes. Early distributors have the opportunity to discuss territorial or channel exclusivity. These terms are negotiated directly with our founders based on distribution network size, geographic reach, and volume capacity. Reach out to discuss specifics.
How does Spark Leaf compare to Red Bull or Monster?
Spark Leaf doesn't compete in the same space. We're a premium, clean-label functional beverage targeting wellness-focused consumers who have outgrown conventional energy drinks. The product is premium-priced, lower-sugar, and positioned for a growing demographic that mainstream brands aren't serving well.
What's the minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities and wholesale pricing are discussed directly based on your distribution capacity and geography. We work with partners of varying scale — from regional specialty distributors to larger national networks. Contact us to discuss what works for your operation.
Is there R&D documentation available?
Yes. We have a full market analysis white paper covering the energy drink and matcha markets, competitive landscape, target demographics, and the product's value proposition. This is available to qualified distribution and investment partners upon request via email.
Why should I act now vs. waiting?
The U.S. market has no dominant matcha energy brand — yet. The category has been proven in Europe (Perfect Ted: $175M valuation in 3 years). The longer you wait, the more crowded the niche becomes and the harder premium shelf positioning becomes to negotiate. Early partners gain the advantages that late entrants have to buy.

Prefer to reach us directly?

No forms required. Our founders respond personally to all calls and emails regarding distribution or investment partnerships.
